Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to authenticate your TikTok account and publish content on your behalf. This includes your TikTok user ID and authentication tokens.

Your information is used solely for the purpose of publishing content to your own TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted at rest. No personal data is stored beyond what is required for the application to function.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share any user data with third parties. The application only communicates with TikTok's official API endpoints.

5. Data Retention

Authentication data is retained only while the application is actively being used. Users can revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
